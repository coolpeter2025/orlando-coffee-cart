Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our service

Simply fill out our booking request form on the contact page. We'll respond within 24-48 hours with availability and a custom quote for your Kissimmee event.

We serve a 60-mile radius from Kissimmee, including Orlando, Celebration, St. Cloud, and all of Osceola County. Custom packages available for locations beyond our standard service area.

We need a flat surface of 6'x8' and access to a 110-volt outlet. If power isn't available, we can provide a generator for an additional fee.

We require a 50% deposit to confirm your booking. Full refunds are available for cancellations made 90+ days before your event, 50% refund for 60-90 days, and no refund within 60 days of your event.
